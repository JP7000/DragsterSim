💡 Physics model: Euler integration at dt=0.0001s. Thrust-proportional CO₂ mass depletion. Dynamic m_eff (chassis + CO₂ + wheel inertia + optional axle inertia). Bearing friction and rolling resistance update each timestep. Best used for comparing designs — not predicting exact times.
